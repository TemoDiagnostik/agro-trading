How We Work (EU Brokerage & Risk-Control)

Euro Agri Trading s.r.o. operates as an independent EU-based brokerage and coordination company. We connect verified suppliers with qualified buyers and support the transaction process in a structured and compliant manner.

Transaction Policy (Risk-Control)

To protect all parties and ensure professional execution, we follow standard international trade practices:

  • Preferred payment: LC at sight (100%) ‚Äî subject to buyer/seller agreement
  • No advance payments
  • Independent inspection at loading: SGS / Intertek / BV (as agreed)
  • Shipment-specific documentation: COA/SGS per shipment; heavy metals (e.g., Cd) upon request/market requirement

Confidentiality & Brokerage Mandate

We follow professional confidentiality when introducing parties. Counterparty details are shared only after receiving a serious request (RFQ/LOI), and we confirm the brokerage mandate in writing (Brokerage Mandate/Agreement) to ensure clarity, transparency, and protection for all parties. Repeat orders/transactions with introduced parties remain covered within the agreed protection term.

Important Note

Euro Agri Trading s.r.o. is not a contracting party to the sales contract between seller and buyer. The sales contract, delivery terms, and performance obligations are agreed directly between the seller and buyer, supported by LC conditions and third-party inspection.
